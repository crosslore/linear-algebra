\begin{ex} Let $V$ be a vector space and suppose $\set{\vect{x}_{1},\cdots ,
\vect{x}_{k}}$ is a set of vectors in $V$. Show that $\vect{0}$
is in $\func{span}\set{\vect{x}_{1},\cdots ,\vect{x}_{k}}$.
\begin{sol}
$\sum_{i=1}^{k}0\vect{x}_{k}=\vect{0}$
\end{sol}
\end{ex}
